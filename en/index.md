    ### data engineering
    
    *Ôªø*{: .fa-solid .fa-gears} designs, implements and manages dozens of

    [Apache Airflow](https://airflow.apache.org/),
    [FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/),

    [Open Source Routing Machine](https://project-osrm.org/) and others

    *Ôªø*{: .fa-solid .fa-gears} implements data flows in collaborative
